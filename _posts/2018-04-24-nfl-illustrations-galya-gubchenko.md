[Galya Chubchenko][1] is an artist focused in the disciplines of digital art, illustrations, and painting. Out of Saint Petersburg, Russia, Chubchenko specializes in creating life-like portraits and visualizations. Chubchenko's portfolio is primarily filled with illustrations of athletes, influential figures, and other notable representations. Earlier this year, Chubchenko published a collection of illustrations as a Super Bowl Preview for *ESPN*. This project includes impressive portraits of players like *Tom Brady*, *Jay Ajayi*, *Blake Bortles*, and other NFL Illustrations. Galya uses a unique blend of shadow and color to create stunning representations of some of the NFL's biggest stars.
